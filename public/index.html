How to Get Your li_at Cookie
  1. Open LinkedIn in Chrome and log in
  2. Press F12 to open Developer Tools
  3. Go to Application tab > Cookies > linkedin.com
  4. Find the cookie named li_at and copy its value
  5. Also find JSESSIONID and copy that too
  6. Paste both values above and click Save
